In the game "Raqb",3 to 6 players compete with 
each other to conquer the country and get the  
territory.
Players often fight each other over the possession 
of the game map provinces . In each of the wars, 
each player increases the strength of his army by
playing soldier cards or confuses other players
by playing special cards.At the end of each battle
the player whose sum of army cards is higher than
the others wins the battle and places his token
in that province . The game ends when one of the
players can take over 3 neighboring provinces or 
5 provinces in the whole map, and that player is 
declared the winner of the game.
